§ 05 — The Clubhouse

Where the dock
becomes a destination.

Post-set recovery isn't an afterthought. It's engineered. Every amenity selected to match the intensity of what you just did on the water.

🚤
2026 Nautique G23 Fleet
Three-boat fleet running Zero-Off GPS speed control, Bose underwater audio, and real-time pull-stat transmission to your dashboard.
🛖
Lakeside Clubhouse
Raw timber and glass architecture. Walk-in cold plunge, infrared sauna, espresso bar with lake views, and a gear vault for every member.
📊
Member Dashboard PWA
Your Zero-Off data, buoy counts, speed history, and video replays all synced to your phone within minutes of stepping off the boat.
🎿
Pro Ski Vault
Connelly, D3, and Radar skis across all sizes and disciplines. Tune bar, binding station, and our certified ski tech on-site weekends.
🌅
Dawn Patrol Program
Priority glass slots from 5–8AM for serious skiers. Zero boat traffic, AI-verified conditions, and a warm breakfast when you're done.
🏆
Pro Coaching
On-call AWSA-certified coaches with in-boat video overlay analysis. AI compares your wake crossing angle to regional top performers in real time.
